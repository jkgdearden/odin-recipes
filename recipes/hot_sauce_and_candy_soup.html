Hot Sauce and Candy Soup!

Easy Recipe for Famous Hot Sauce and Candy Soup

It's midnight, and as you ruthessly plow down hordes of Covenant in Halo, you suddenly feel rumbling... It's your stomach and you forgot to eat dinner! But the predicament remains, what do you eat? You can't skip dinner, but it's also too late to eat dinner and then eat dessert, and you absolutely CANNOT skip dessert! So what do you do?? Combine them!

Chow down on your favorite candy swimming in your favorite hot sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1 large bottle of your favorite hot sauce
  • Any variety of your favorite candy
  • Garlic
  • Soup crackers
  • milk
  • water
  • white bread

Steps

  1. Fill a microwave safe bowl 1/2 full with your favorite hot sauce.
  2. Heat up the hot suace in the microwave until it's just right, not too cold, not too hot. This is also known as, "warm".
  3. Pour your favorite candies into bowl until the liquid reaches the top.
  4. Add garlic to taste
  5. Add soup crackers for additional crunch and enjoy!

You might find that as yummy as this dish is, it is also very spicy. Use the last 3 listed ingredeients to calm down the fire. (We find that white bread works the best; to each their own.)
