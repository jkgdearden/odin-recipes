Cereal!

A Cassic...

Great for every ocassion.... when you wake up, for dinner, after sex.....Whenever you want!

It's so simple, even you can make it!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal
  • Bowl
  • Spoon
  • Napkin

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into bowl
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. With the Spoon, lift cereal and milk to mouth; proceed to chew
  4. Wipe beard with napkin. Repeat!
