Quacamole

Easy Recipe for Tasty Quacamole

Guacamole is basically mashed, raw avocados mixed with other ingredients to taste. The avocados give guacamole the green base, and other ingredients, such as tomatoes and onions give it some textured flavor. Guacamole is especially popular on Super Bowl Sunday and Cinco de Mayo. This treat is a perfect tailgate or party snack and a favorite Mexican restaurant side dish. Guacamole, due to its base of avocados are also super healthy. Guacamole has been linked to improving heart, skin, and hair health, as well as contributing to weight loss, cancer prevention, and a stronger immune and digestive system

There are hundreds of different guacamole recipes across the world. In the southeast, specifically Atlanta, the most popular recipe calls for combining the following ingredients:

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Chopped Onions
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Tapatio
  • Lime Juice
  • Minced Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and mash avocados in a medium serving bowl.
  2. Stir in onion, garlic, tomato, lime juice, salt and pepper. Add all ingredients to taste.
  3. Season with remaining lime juice and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Chill for half an hour to blend flavors.
